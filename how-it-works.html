How it works

Discover what wins.

Votage is a social voting arena. Users create battles, choose a side, vote with USDC and collect rewards in WIN. The product is built around opinion, conviction and participation.

What is Votage?

Votage is a social platform where battles between two options compete for attention and value. Each vote moves real value through the protocol. Users can create battles, vote with USDC and receive WIN-based rewards when they are on the winning side.

How to create a battle

1. Connect your wallet.
2. Open Start Battle.
3. Upload two images, set labels and choose one or two categories.
4. Select duration from 1 to 7 days.
5. Confirm the on-chain transaction.

How to vote

1. Pick a live battle.
2. Open the vote modal.
3. Review the selected side, image and current vote quote.
4. Approve USDC if required and confirm the transaction.
5. Vote again any time if you want more exposure.

Badges and multipliers

Badges do not mint tokens directly. They increase airdrop weighting inside the season framework. Hold, creator, player, performance, loyalty, social and DeFi badges are all part of progression.

FAQ

1. Is Votage gambling?No. The platform is built around social choice, opinion and competitive participation.
2. Why do I vote with USDC?USDC removes friction because it avoids forcing a swap before first participation.
3. Why are rewards in WIN?WIN is the protocol token and captures long-term value created by usage.
4. Do I need to hold WIN?No, but holding WIN unlocks the holder badge path.
5. Can I vote more than once?Yes. If you want larger exposure, you can vote again as long as the battle remains open.
6. What resets every season?Season points and ranking reset. Badge ownership remains in your profile history.
7. Do badges expire?No. Once unlocked, a badge stays in your badge vault.
8. How is the feed ranked?The feed is not chronological. It prioritizes value, recency and time remaining.
9. Can I upload any image?You may only upload content you have the right to use.
10. Does testnet activity have monetary value?No. Testnet is for product validation and community traction.
