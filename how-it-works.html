How it works

Discover what wins.

Votage is a social voting arena. Users create battles, choose a side and move value through participation. Gas and voting friction stay low because the current deployed battle contract accepts USDC, while rewards flow in WIN.

Protocol flow

1. Create a battle on-chain with a duration from 1 to 7 days.
2. Users vote with USDC on either side.
3. The battle resolves when time ends.
4. 74% goes to winners, 10% to the creator, 5% to treasury, 5% to burn, 6% to the airdrop pool.
5. Winners claim in WIN.

FAQ

Is this gambling?
No. Battles are collective decision flows around opinions, brands, culture and crypto narratives.

Why USDC in and WIN out?
It removes swap friction at the start while still tying rewards to the protocol token.

Does testnet value carry over?
No direct monetary value carries over. Testnet is for product validation and community traction.

Legal notes

This build is a testnet product layer. Nothing on testnet represents guaranteed financial value. Contract logic and token distribution may change before mainnet launch. Always review local legal requirements and launchpad rules before public fundraising or token generation.
